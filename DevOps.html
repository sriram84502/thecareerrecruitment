DevOps represents a paradigm shift in the world of software development and IT operations. It's a holistic approach that combines development (Dev) and operations (Ops) into a collaborative, iterative, and automated process. The goal is to bridge the gap between traditionally siloed development and operations teams, fostering a culture of collaboration and shared responsibility. By breaking down organizational barriers and promoting efficient communication and cooperation, DevOps aims to enhance the software development lifecycle, from planning and coding to testing, deployment, and monitoring.

As technology continues to evolve, DevOps is expected to remain a critical approach in the software development landscape. With the rise of cloud computing, microservices architecture, and the increasing complexity of applications, DevOps will play an even more pivotal role in enabling organizations to adapt and scale efficiently. The integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) into DevOps practices is also anticipated, offering predictive analytics for better decision-making and further automation of tasks. Ultimately, the future of DevOps lies in continued innovation and integration of emerging technologies to streamline processes, enhance collaboration, and drive software delivery to new heights.

Starting Beginners Level Course

DevOps is an entry point for individuals looking to venture into the dynamic field of development and operations integration. This course provides foundational knowledge and a gentle introduction to the principles, practices, and tools that constitute the DevOps methodology. Participants learn about the collaborative culture, agile methodologies, version control, and basic automation practices. The curriculum is designed to ensure that beginners comprehend the core concepts and philosophies that underpin DevOps, setting a strong footing for further exploration.

  • Linux Basics and Virtualisation
  • Shell Scripting and Adv Linux Networking concepts
  • Cloud Basics [AWS/Azure]
  • Exploring IAAS, PAAS, StAAS, SAAS and DbAAS in Cloud with real world projects.
  • Ansible
  • Vesion Control with Git and Github
  • Docker
  • Kubernetes
